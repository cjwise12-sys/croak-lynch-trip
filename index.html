Scotland

Edinburgh Local Finds What locals would actually suggest
Pubs & Bars
  • Sandy Bell's 📍 — Forrest Rd tiny folk music pub, no stage — musicians just show up and play in the corner · completely unpretentious, zero tourists · one of the best pub experiences in Edinburgh
  • The Hanging Bat 📍 — Lothian Rd craft beer bar with an extraordinary tap list · not whisky, not tartan — just great beer and a relaxed crowd
Restaurants
  • Dishoom 📍 — St Andrew Square Bombay café food, incredible breakfast, always buzzing · locals love it · technically a chain but the Edinburgh outpost is outstanding
  • The Kitchin 📍 — The Shore, Leith one of Scotland's best restaurants · 15 min cab from Old Town
Neighbourhoods
  • Leith 📍 Edinburgh's old port district, now genuinely cool · The Shore has excellent restaurants and bars along the water · feels like a completely different city · 15 min cab from Old Town
  • Grassmarket Market 📍 on Saturdays — small, local, right outside the Wilde Aparthotels door
  • Stockbridge Sunday Market 📍 — Stockbridge on Sundays only · local producers, street food, artisan goods · charming neighbourhood feel · 10 min cab from Old Town · note: no Sunday falls during the Edinburgh portion of this trip (Aug 12–15), so this would require a special detour or doesn't fit this itinerary
Quiet & Different
  • Dr Neil's Garden 📍 — Duddingston, foot of Arthur's Seat free, quiet garden on Duddingston Loch, created over 60 years by two local doctors · swans glide past a 12th-century church · on a weekday morning you may have it entirely to yourself · genuinely different from the pub/restaurant scene — a peaceful counterpoint
  • The Sheep Heid Inn 📍 — Duddingston reputedly Scotland's oldest surviving pub · old country inn character, very different from the Old Town pubs · pair with Dr Neil's Garden and a Duddingston-side approach up Arthur's Seat, quieter than the Holyrood route
